FinWise PH – Website Plan

Site Name

FinWise PH

The name represents a Philippine-focused financial guidance platform that helps users make smart, cost-efficient decisions when transferring money, using e-wallets, digital banks, and international services like Wise. It reflects financial literacy and fee-saving strategies.

Site Purpose

FinWise PH aims to guide Filipino users in choosing the best payment routes, banking tools, e-wallets, and international transfer options. The site will help users reduce or avoid fees, compare digital banking options, and provide step-by-step instructions for cost-efficient transactions.

Scenarios

  • How do I send money from Wise to GCash or a PH bank with the lowest fees?
  • Which digital bank should I use if I want high interest and zero transfer charges?
  • What’s the cheapest way to pay for international subscriptions like Spotify or Netflix?
  • How can I avoid hidden fees when transferring money between Philippine e-wallets?

Color Scheme

Primary Colors:

  • #38bdf8 Sky Blue – Used for headings, accents, links, and highlights.
  • #0b1620 Dark Navy – Main background color for a clean, modern dark theme.
  • #e2e8f0 Light Gray – Body text color for readability.

The same scheme is applied to this document.

Typography

  • Inter – Used for headers, titles, and section labels.
  • Roboto – Used for all body text and descriptions.

Both fonts provide a clean, modern, and readable UI for a financial website.
